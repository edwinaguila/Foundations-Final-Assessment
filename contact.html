About Me

Edwin Aguila, also known as EA, is a proud Puerto Rican from East New York, Brooklyn, NY. His passions are two-fold as an actor and music artist. From participating in the Lyricist Lounge Rhyme Sketch Program to creating his own event Coquito Coro, he makes sure to represent! Recently, he started writing lyrics and recording videos again on Instagram.
